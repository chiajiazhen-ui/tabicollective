About Tabi

Tabi Collective began from a curiosity for slow travel and meaningful connections. We design trips that spend more time in fewer places — so you can sink into local rhythms and return refreshed.

Our values

  • Small groups, low-impact travel.
  • Community-first itineraries and local partnerships.
  • Thoughtful pacing and wellbeing-led experiences.
