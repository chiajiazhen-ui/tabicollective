Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

Our Commitment

At Tabi Collective, we believe that discretion is a fundamental part of a luxury experience. In accordance with the Singapore Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA), we are committed to protecting your personal data and ensuring that your journey with us remains private and secure.

Data Collection

To curate your tailored experiences, we may collect information such as your name, contact details, passport information (for travel bookings), and personal preferences. This data is collected only when voluntarily provided through our inquiry forms or during the consultation process.

Purpose of Use

Your data is used solely to:

  • Design and fulfill bespoke travel itineraries and M.I.C.E. solutions.
  • Communicate essential updates regarding your bookings.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements in the destinations you visit.

Disclosure to Third Parties

We only share your information with trusted partners (such as boutique hotels, local guides, or airlines) when necessary to execute your itinerary. We do not sell your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.
